About

We aim to create sustainable living environments with heart and soul.

Bothly Corp is a start up firm. It was formerly called Apple Engineering Group, which was founded in June, 2012. Major business was surveying, property subdivision and legal description, grading and drainage, and street improvement.

We have put together strong professional team members who own engineering degrees in the US, Germany, and other countries in engineering field and Business Management and with over twenty plus year’s excellent services.
